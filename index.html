What are "Tools of Freedom"

Tools of Freedom, is a podcast dedicated to exploring new technologies and ideas that allow all peoples to build new and better economic systems outside of the reach of regulations, taxation, and arbitrary laws. Be believe in the power of new technologies to expand to the rights and soverignty of all human beings regardless of race, religion, culture, and geographical location.

Why do we need better tools for freedom.

What Is Freedom and why do we need it.

The concept of freedom is well defined by the Non-Aggression Principle which states that any human being has the near absolute right do whatever they please as long as the do not initiate violence upon and other individual human being. This is based in the concept of property rights.
